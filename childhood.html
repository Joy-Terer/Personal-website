Early Life

Growing up , I had a wonderful childhood filled with happy memories, family experience and also painful moments.

My family has always been a significant part of my life, teaching me the importance of exceling in my studies and also working smart and not just hard.

I grew up listening to all those pieces of parental advice. To this day, I still receive them, especially from my mom and grandma.

Generally for me growing up was hard especially when i had to transition from being the 'baby of the family' to a second born . But having family with me has always been a blessing.
