Educational Journey

School & Learning

I attended Kapsoit Junior Academy for my primary education, where I attained my KCPE certificate. I also gained basic computer skills.

In 2019, I joined Tengecha Girls High School in Kapkatet, where I sat for my KCSE exams after four years.

During high school, I was part of the school's basketball team and an active member of the journalism club. I also honed my computer skills and expanded my knowledge in various fields, including mathematics.

Later on, I joined Zetech University, where I am currently a third-year student pursuing a degree in Software Engineering.
